Saturnin

Saturnin is a family of lightweight symmetric algorithms (authenticated encryption and hashing) that was submitted to the NIST project for lightweight cryptography. It reached the second round of the competition.

  • Saturnin: A suite of Lightweight Symmetric Algorithms for Post-quantum Security - A. Canteaut, S. Duval, G. Leurent, M. Naya-Plasencia, L. Perrin, T. Pornin and A. Schrottenloher - second-round candidate to the NIST competition for lightweight cryptography. [link]
